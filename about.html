If you're wondering what the image is on the Home page, it's the recently invented blue LED by 3 Japanese physicists who won the Nobel Prize in Physics. Red and green LEDs have existed for a long time and blue was the only one that scientists didn't know how to make. As a result, the recent creation of this LED led to the completion of red, blue and green. With this, highly efficient white light can be produced to light up our homes. Why is this important? LEDs are extremely energy efficient because it can convert most of the electricity into light and they last forever (approximately 100,000 hours, to be accurate).
